Journals & Series
International Journal of Algebra and Computation
Managing Editor

Publishes high quality original research papers in combinatorial, algorithmic and computational aspects of algebra, including combinatorial and geometric group theory, semigroup theory, algorithmic aspects of universal algebra, computational commutative algebra, probabilistic models related to algebraic structures, and random algebraic structures.

SpringerBriefs in Mathematics
Editorial Board

Concise volumes of 50–125 pages covering cutting-edge research and practical applications across mathematics. SpringerBriefs in Mathematics showcases expositions in all areas of mathematics and applied mathematics, from new results in classical fields to emerging topics and applications.

Semigroup Forum
Editorial Board

A platform for the speedy and efficient transmission of information on current research in semigroup theory, covering algebraic semigroups, topological semigroups, partially ordered semigroups, transformation semigroups, semigroups of operators, and applications to ring theory, category theory, automata, and logic.

Discussiones Mathematicae — General Algebra and Applications
Editorial Board

A high-quality refereed journal devoted to Classical and General Algebra with applications to Computer Science and Combinatorics, publishing original papers and occasional authoritative survey articles.
